Emily Weil

Founder of Girls Vibe Out

Emily Weil is a digital creator who lives with her husband and two kids in New York City. She has worked at startups including web and crypto ventures, and has created digital artwork for galleries and retail spaces.

Girls Vibe Out grew out of her desire to make AI less intimidating — and more useful — to people who might not think it's for them.

She's also published an e-book AI for Moms, a practical, no-jargon guide to using ChatGPT in everyday life.
